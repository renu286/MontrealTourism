Find the Perfect
Place to stay

When it comes to accommodations in Montreal, there’s no lack of options to choose from.
Luxury hotels, quaint bed & breakfasts—Montreal has lodgings for every budget and taste.

If you love living in the lap of luxury, Montreal’s downtown core is home to several
lavish hotels with spectacular views of the city.

Looking to experience Montreal from a cozier, more intimate setting? You might be
interested in one of the city’s charming bed & breakfasts. Don’t want to stray too far
from the airport? Choose between several hotels located right next to Montreal Pearson
International Airport.

Wherever you decide to stay, you’ll have access to plenty of unforgettable attractions and
events that will guarantee you have the trip of a lifetime.
